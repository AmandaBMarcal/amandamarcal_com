A little about Amanda!

Amanda is a passionate adventurist and a real problem solver. As a full-stack web developer, she enjoys writing code for the betterment and progression of the project she is working on and or company she is working with. As a Boston native, she works alongside businesses creating software solutions and applications while assisting on business continuity. In her free time, she mentors at Resilient Coders. RC being the starting point of her programming carreer, she spends hours assisting and passing her knowledge to others so that they may have the same passion that she has in coding.
